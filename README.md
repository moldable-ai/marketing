# Moldable: Personal software. Built for change.

**Your personal operating system for software that doesn't exist yet.**

Moldable is for people who refuse to settle for rigid, one-size-fits-all software. Instead of buying apps or waiting for someone to build what you need, you describe it â€” and Moldable creates it.

## The Vision

Software today is built for the masses, not the individual. It's often rigid, expensive, and disconnected from your actual workflow. Moldable inverts this model:

- **Software is summoned**: Describe your need in natural language, and a personal AI agent builds it instantly.
- **Software is shaped**: Evolve your apps through conversation. If your workflow changes, your software changes with it.
- **Software is discarded**: When a tool has served its purpose, discard it. Creating is so effortless that nothing needs to be permanent.

## Core Principles

### 1. Your Apps, Your Data
Everything runs locally on your machine. Connect to AI services when you choose, but your code and data live on your computer, not someone else's servers.
